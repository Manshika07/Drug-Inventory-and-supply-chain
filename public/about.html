Empowering Healthcare: End-to-End Drug Supply Management

ABOUT


The "Drug Inventory and Supply Chain Tracking System" aims to streamline and optimize the procurement,
distribution, and consumption of drugs in hospitals and medical institutions.
It ensures that the right drug reaches the right people in the right condition, quantity,
and at the right cost at the right time. The system provides real-time online monitoring
through a dashboard and tracks vendor activities from supply orders to shipment, ensuring
a seamless supply chain for drugs. The goal is to increase efficiency and ensure constant drug availability.
